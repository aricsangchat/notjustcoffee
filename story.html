Our Story

We invite you to taste first — without knowing the details. Only after that, reveal the lot. This approach comes from a coffee lab background where blind cuppings were always the starting point.

Why “Not Just Coffee”

Coffee is a portal. For most people it’s simply caffeine — but in truth, coffee is far greater. It holds the labor of families, their hands, their care for the land — and the quiet intelligence of process.

Words from the Founder, Natalia Sangchat

The tasting process itself is a ritual. When approached consciously, it enhances the quality of life — and the quality of your presence within it.

The portal opens when you realize: this is not just coffee. When you feel your connection to the Earth through every sip — and your connection to yourself.
